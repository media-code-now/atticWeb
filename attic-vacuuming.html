Attic Vacuuming/Air Duct Cleaning

The first step in achieving clean air in your attic is professionally vacuuming every single part of the attic floor. LV Attic Pros uses 14hp and 27hp Vortex vacuums to make your attic floors look like new and ready for new insulation. We have 3 different diameter hoses to reach the most tough spots of your attic and ensure the most clean environment possible.

When removing older, inefficient insulation, professional vacuuming is the only way to go. After years of moldy build up, there’s really no way of knowing how much dust, dirt and bacteria will come out of the insulation we remove.

Properly removing insulation and cleaning your attic is a job that should be left to professionals! Complications can occur when the insulation is just pulled or yanked out and can be extremely expensive to repair and fix. There may be hazardous materials under the insulation that you’re not even aware of that would need further attention such as asbestos, older fiberglass products, etc. A job of this sort requires professional cleaning equipment and personal protective equipment that isn’t always found in the neighborhood hardware store.

Although it may be tempting to do this complicated project yourself, it is always best to call a qualified professional to prevent probable expensive damage to your home or even worse - an accident that leads to a serious injury!
