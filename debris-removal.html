Silver Radiant Barrier Installation

The absolute most efficient way to maintain stable temperatures in your home during the long Las Vegas summer and even the short, cold winters is by installing a Silver Radiant Barrier in your attic.

This groundbreaking technology is energy efficient, easy to install, is considerably less expensive than foam insulation and improves HVAC performance by up to 50%!

Composed of multiple layers of low emmitance material sheets designed to reduce radiant heat transfer, the LV Attic Pros Silver Radiant Barrier is very thin when initially installing and will expand to form a heat-shield in your attic which will cause a more comfortable environment in the home itself as the heat doesnâ€™t penetrate through.

This new and advanced way to insulate your attic reflects 97% of the heat in the roof itself, leaving your HVAC systems very little to do during those hot summer days.
