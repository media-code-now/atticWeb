What kind of hazards are in my roof that I should be aware of?

Anything from rodents to flammable debris could be laying around your attic without you even knowing about it. Since electrical hazards can be very dangerous, we inspect for loose unattached wires and open electrical boxes and will recommend a qualified, licensed professional to resolve any kind of electrical issue. Hazard inspection is important to do at least once a year to remove any kind of threat.

What areas do you service?

LV Attic Pros service the whole city of Las Vegas, North Las Vegas, Henderson, Boulder City and the entire area of Clark County.

What are the benefits of installing the silver radiant barrier?

Installing the brand new silver radiant barrier is the most efficient way to reduce your energy bills as well as keep your attic clean and cool throughout the year. Reducing the temperature by up to 30° at all times, it also helps keep the cool air in your house so that your air conditioner doesn’t work extra hard during those blazing summer days.

How do I know what my attic needs?

In most cases, you don’t! That’s what you have professionals for. LV Attic Pros has experienced technicians that will be glad to meet with you for a quick inspection and give you a free quote based on what your home really needs!

Does my attic have anything to do with energy efficiency?

Your attic has almost EVERYTHING to do with energy efficiency. If you have poor insulation, collected debris, dirt and dust everywhere you look - we can lower your energy costs, guaranteed. Once your attic is squeaky clean and has the proper insulation or radiant barrier installed, your energy costs will reduce dramatically. Making sure your air conditioning unit doesn’t need to work twice as hard is what we’re all about!

How often do I need to clean my attic?

If you’re not professionally trained for this - we don’t recommend you do it on your own. You would need a professional cleaning and inspection done once a year in order to catch whatever hazards may be laying around before they cause any further damage. Most attics’ air ventilation areas are often blocked or clogged, not allowing fresh clean air to circulate through the attic. We remove the debris and blockage from these areas to result in proper ventilation throughout your attic.
